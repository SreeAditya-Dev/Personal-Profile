Personal
Profile

"Hello! I am V Sree Aditya, formerly a student of D.A.V. School, Adambakkam. I recently completed my 12th standard and am now eagerly looking ahead. Over the past four years, my journey with computers has been nothing short of captivating, igniting a passion for various sectors within computer science. It all began during the three-month break after my class 10 exams, where I delved into the realms of visual design and web development. However, my fascination with Artificial Intelligence traces back to my 9th standard when I first encountered it as part of our school curriculum. This initial exposure planted a seed that blossomed into a deep-rooted interest in the field. In my school, we explored various subjects, including Artificial Intelligence, which further fueled my passion. Ultimately, my biggest aspiration is to bridge connections among people by constructing a vibrant web community, akin to LinkedIn, in the future.

"My education at D.A.V Adambakkam, from pre-KG to 8th grade, was filled with memorable moments. Mornings began at 7:00 am, rushing to school by 8:10 am amidst gentle reminders from my mother and daily chores. School days spanned from 9:30 am until 3:45 pm, including morning assembly, breaks, and lunch. Despite occasional tardiness, each day held its own charm, especially during short breaks when we indulged in snacks.

After the 8th standard exams, COVID-19 abruptly altered our lives. Amidst rumors and confinement, online classes became the new norm for my secondary education. Despite initial challenges, I embraced this shift, even joining math tuition under the guidance of N. Balaji sir, who became a pivotal mentor. With determination, I tackled the lethargy induced by virtual learning, gearing up for the challenges of 10th grade. As I navigated through the uncertainties, I held onto my resolve to excel. Now, standing on the threshold of 12th grade, I reflect on the journey that shaped me. Each hurdle became a stepping stone, each setback a lesson learned. Through it all, I've grown, both academically and personally.

As I embark on the final leg of this educational odyssey, I am reminded of a quote : 'In the midst of chaos, there is also opportunity.' - Sun Tzu"
